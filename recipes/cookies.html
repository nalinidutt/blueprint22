Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 4 tb peanut butter/almond butter
  • ¼ cup honey or sugar free maple syrup
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ milk
  • Toppings of choice (almonds, cashews, etc.)

Directions

  • Mix and form into balls
  • Bake for 12-15 minutes at 250 F and enjoy!
