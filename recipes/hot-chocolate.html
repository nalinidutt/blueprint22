Hot Chocolate

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • Water

Directions

  • In a mug, mix 1 tsp cocoa powder, 2 tsp sugar, and 2 tb hot water till thoroughly combined
  • Heat up milk to desired temperature.
  • Add the chocolate mixture, mix, and enjoy!
