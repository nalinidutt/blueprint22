Fries

Ingredients

  • 5 potatoes
  • Paprika, pepper, salt, garlic powder (1 tsp each)
  • 1 tsp of baking powder
  • 2 tsp of olive oil

Directions

  • Cut a potato into 2 inch long pieces
  • Dry potatoes very well
  • Coat the potatoes in the seasonings and baking powder
  • Air fry or bake at 450 for 20 minutes
