Red Sauce Pasta

Ingredients

  • ½ cup standard marinara sauce
  • ½ tb butter
  • Garlic powder
  • Pasta and veggies of your choice

Directions

  • Mix marinara, butter and garlic powder in a heated pan
  • Add in boiled pasta noodles and veggies of choice in the saucepan. Enjoy!
