Brownie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup oat flour
  • ¼ cup cocoa powder
  • ⅓ cup honey
  • 1 tb coconut oil
  • 5 tb yogurt
  • 1 tb baking soda
  • ½ tb cinnamon
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ cup milk
  • 3 tb coconut flakes
  • ¼ cup of dark chocolate chips

Directions

  • Mix in a bowl.
  • Empty in a brownie baking sheet when ingredients are fully combined.
  • Bake at 350 F for 13 minutes
  • Leave to cool for 5-10 minutes. Enjoy!
