Chicken Tenders/Nuggets

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • 1 egg
  • Â¾ cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 tsp paprika, salt, pepper and garlic powder each
  • 1 cup of breadcrumbs

Directions

  • Slice up chicken breast into 2 inch pieces for tenders and 1 inch pieces for nuggets
  • For egg wash, beat one egg, and add salt and pepper to taste
  • Mix flour and seasonings for flour bath
  • Dip chicken pieces in egg wash, flour bath, and breadcrumbs
  • Bake at 400 for 13 minutes on each side. Enjoy!t
