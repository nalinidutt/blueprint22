Quesadilla

Ingredients

  • Whole Wheat Tortilla
  • 1 tb sour cream
  • ¼ cup cheese of your choice
  • ¼ cup of peppers
  • ¼ cup of protein of choice
  • 1tb jalapenos
  • ⅛ cup tomatoes

Directions

  • Spread sourcream on the tortilla
  • Add cheese, peppers, protein, jalapenos, and tomatoes
  • Cook for 2 minutes on a pan on both sides, enjoy!
