Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • ½ cup green peppers
  • 1 large carrot
  • ½ cup onion
  • ½ cup peas
  • 4 cup rice
  • 2 tb chill sauce
  • 1tb soy sauce
  • 2 tb vinegar
  • 1 tb sweet and sour sauce

Directions

  • Cook your rice - 4 cups
  • Add ½ cup green peppers, 1 large carrot, ½ cup onion, ½ cup peas
  • Cook for 5 - 8 minutes
  • Cook for 5 - 8 minutes
  • Add soy sauce, vinegar, chill sauce, sweet and sour sauce
  • Mix the sauce and veggies and add rice
  • Enjoy!
