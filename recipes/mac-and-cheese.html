Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 cloves garlic or 1 tsp garlic powder '
  • 2 tb olive oil
  • 1/2 cup of mexican cheese (or cheddar cheese)'
  • 1 cup of mozzarella cheese
  • 2 Tb whole wheat flour
  • ½ cup reduced fat
  • Pasta

Directions

  • Boil elbow pasta as written on the package. Add 2 tb of salt to the boiling water immediately after adding the pasta.
  • For the sauce, add these to a pan…
    • 2 cloves garlic or 1 tsp garlic powder
    • 2 tb olive oil
    • 1/2 cup of mexican cheese (or cheddar cheese)
    • ½ cup of mozzarella cheese
    • 2 Tb whole wheat flour
  • Whisk after adding the whole wheat flour little by little
  • Add ½ cup reduced fat milk little by little and keep mixing
  • Add cheese to thicken (approx ¼ - ½ cup)
  • Keep whisking till it reaches a sauce like consistency
  • Add noodles and enjoy!
