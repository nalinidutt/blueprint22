Alfredo Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 container of spinach
  • 2 cloves of garlic or 1 tsp of garlic powder
  • 2 tb olive oil
  • ½ cup of cream cheese
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese

Directions

  • Cook the spinach with garlic and olive oil until the spinach has reduced in size
  • In a pan, mix and add ¼ cup milk slowly until it reaches a sauce like consistency
  • Add the spinach, oregano(1 tsp), basil (1 tsp), and red pepper flakes(1 tsp)
  • Add in boiled pasta noodles of choice
  • Mix well and enjoy!
