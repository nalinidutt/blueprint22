Burgers

Ingredients

  • Black Beans (1 cup)
  • Whole Wheat Burger Buns
  • Garlic
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Condiments of Choice
  • Olive Oil

Directions

  • Soak black beans in a bowl of warm water until they are fork ready
  • In a bowl, combine cooked black beans, salt, pepper, paprika, and cut garlic
  • Combine the beans with the spices until the beans have loosened up a bit, but are not completely mashed
  • Create black bean patties to desired size
  • In a skillet, warm up olive oil and place the patties in
  • Flip the patties until both sides reach a crispy exterior
  • Assemble your burger and enjoy!
